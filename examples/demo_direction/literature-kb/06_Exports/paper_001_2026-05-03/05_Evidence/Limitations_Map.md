# Limitations map

Publicly acknowledged limitations of methods in this direction, drawn from
the papers themselves (their own "Limitations" sections, follow-up
critiques, or survey syntheses).

Each entry:

- **Limitation** (one sentence, phrased as the literature phrases it).
- **Affected method(s)** (link to `04_Concepts/Methods/<slug>.md` or to
  representative Papers/).
- **Source(s)**: [Paper](../02_Literature/Papers/<id>.md) -- section.
- **Attempts to address**: list of later papers that aim at this limitation.

Do not write your own speculative limitations here; speculation belongs in
a workspace.
