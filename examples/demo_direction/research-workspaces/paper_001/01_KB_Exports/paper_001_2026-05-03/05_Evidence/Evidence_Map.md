# Evidence map

Consolidated view of the direction's empirical claims and their supporting
papers. Every claim must resolve to one or more paper cards already in
`02_Literature/Papers/`.

## Structure

Each entry follows this shape:

```
- **Claim** (one sentence).
  - Evidence: [Paper](../02_Literature/Papers/<id>.md) -- section / table / figure.
  - Counter-evidence (if any): [Paper](../02_Literature/Papers/<id2>.md).
  - Last reviewed: YYYY-MM-DD.
```
