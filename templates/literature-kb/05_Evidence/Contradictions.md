# Contradictions

Where two or more papers in this KB make incompatible empirical claims.

Each entry:

- **Question** (what the papers disagree about).
- **Claim A**: [Paper](../02_Literature/Papers/<id-a>.md).
- **Claim B**: [Paper](../02_Literature/Papers/<id-b>.md).
- **Possible reconciliation** (drawn from later literature, if any).
- **Status**: open / resolved-by <paper> / context-dependent.

Contradictions are valuable. Do not hide them. Do not resolve them with
your own unpublished experiments; that belongs in a workspace.
